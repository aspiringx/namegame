# NameGame.app
Relationships start with names

_“You wanna be where everybody knows your name.” ~ Cheers _

NameGame makes it fun for people in a group to meet, remember names, and get acquainted. 

## How to Play

* Create a group
* Add your name and pic
* Greet people by letting them scan your code

**Greet** someone by showing your NameGame code. They scan it with their phone camera and instantly see your name and pic in the album. No need to download an app or register. 

New players can stay **anonymous**, only showing their first name (no pic) and only visible to those who directly greet them. No pressure when they’re just checking your group out. If they like it, they can add their pic and become visible to the group. 

Like a **scavenger hunt**, you see photos of people in the group, but only the names of those you’ve greeted. To unlock their names and connect, just greet. NameGame is your fun excuse. 

If you’re a little shy, no problem. Just sit back and let the extroverts do their thing. 

When you create a group, enter an approximate number of members. If your group has 50 people, NameGame will indicate this, motivating players to greet and include everyone. 

Later, you can review your album to remember names, **chat** (in game) with people you’ve greeted, etc. A nice way to stay in touch with people you’ve just met without the awkwardness of asking them for contact info. 

When entering a new group, people want to understand how/where they might fit in. NameGame makes it easy to **learn more** about the group, **sub-groups** that might interest them, or **chat** to ask questions, etc. **Anyone can create sub-groups**, allowing people to connect around shared interests.
